# Phase 4 - Automation and Continuous Improvement

## Objective

Close the loop from detection to response to tuning, with AWX workflows and monthly improvement cycles.

## Outcomes

- Controlled event-to-action orchestration
- Drift detection and scheduled remediation
- Resilience testing and restore confidence

## Scope

- AWX workflow chains with approvals
- Monthly posture review and alert tuning
- Backup/restore drills and verification

## Deliverables

- AWX workflow templates with approval nodes
- Monthly reliability/security report job
- Runbook updates from incident learnings

## Exit Criteria

- Detect-to-respond workflow validated in controlled scenarios
- Restore drill passes and is repeatable
- Monthly review yields measurable improvements
